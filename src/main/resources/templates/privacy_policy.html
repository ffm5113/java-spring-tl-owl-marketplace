On the Web Listing (OWL) Marketplace

Privacy Policy - November 2022


Voluntary Use & Privacy Notification
The web application can be used without revealing data. You may be asked to provide
personal information such as your name, email address, and physical address so that
we can build your account profile and maintain relevant data. OWL Marketplace will
never sell, share, or exchange your personal data. In the event that your data is
requested by law enforcement, we will comply under the jurisdiction of the law.

EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a privacy and security law enacted
by the European Union (EU) in 2018. The GDPR provides European Union residents
with safeguards based on 7 principles of data protection and 8 privacy rights.
According to the statute, any organization that processes personal data of people
in the EU is compelled to comply with the regulations, which include the
implementation of "data protection by design and by default".

If you are a European Union resident, in some circumstances you may have the right
to access your personal information, make corrections, restrict processing of your
information, and object to certain marketing communications or data profiles. As per
the GDPR, EU residents are entitled to file complaints with either a regional or a
national data protection authority.

OWL Marketplace app data is stored in the United States via an Azure MySQL database.
In providing your personal information to OWL Marketplace, you consent to storage
of personal data within the United States.

NOTE: European Economic Area (EEA) has separate jurisdiction from the United States.

For information about General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), please visit:
https://gdpr.eu
